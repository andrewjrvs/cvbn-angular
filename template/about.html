Hello

We live in amazing times, but with each new technology evolution, our jobs may become more complex. Technology is important, but it must be made functional and useful to provide users with a product that is not only present, but also beneficial and productive. My passion is merging technology and usefulness.

My job experience is broad, allowing me to consider functionality from both implementer and user perspectives. While working in data entry, I wrote a script to automatically enter basic data, allowing the department to streamline efforts and focus on items needing more specific attention. When in a call center I was instrumental in moving the department off of our old scripts and onto PowerShell. In addition, I simplified call logging by integrating it into our employee look-up script, which in turn saved time after each call.

Since working as a programmer, I have continued to bring technology to users to increase efficiency and simplify their tasks in large and small ways. These include creating the option to copy/paste in some forms to save employees from having to re-type information, including type-ahead on some fields and when appropriate allowing the option for information look-up. Whatever the job, I want to help make technology an even greater asset to the user.
